Who may reserve a room in SEAS buildings?
  • SEAS faculty, staff, and students
  • Other Harvard University faculty, staff, and students will need to be sponsored by a SEAS Faculty member or SEAS senior administration member.

Due to academic scheduling, EMS allows you to reserve rooms 7 days prior to your event up until August 31st 2013. Please contact or falls on a holiday if you need a reservation beyond that date or if your event falls on a holiday.

Directions on how to reserve a room:


Once you have chosen a room, click on the green plus sign to reserve it (see example below):




If you do not need a reservation anymore, please cancel the reservations by scrolling over "Reservations" in the menu above and select "View My Reservations", then selecting the reservation you want to Cancel and finally clicking on the link that says "Cancel Booking".
Please note that if an event is due in 3 business days or less, you will need to contact events@seas.harvard.edu to request the cancellation.


If you need to do a teleconference, please verify that the room you select has phone jacks and that it has a phone number assigned to it by clicking on the Room name and seeing its details. Some of the rooms do not have phone jacks enabled or no phone jacks at all. You can show all the rooms that have enabled phone jacks by selecting "Telephone Jacks" in the "Availability Filters" section in the left and clicking Find Space.
Most of the rooms do not have the teleconferencing phone in them, you have to request separately and in advance to SEAS Computing at help@seas.harvard.edu.
