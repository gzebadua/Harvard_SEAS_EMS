How do I get started with EMS?

Due to academic scheduling, EMS allows you to reserve rooms 7 days prior to your event up until August 31st 2013. If your event requires more (or less) advanced planning or falls on a holiday, please contact events@seas.harvard.edu.

Please note that academic programming takes priority. To start, click on "Log In" in the menu above and provide your SEAS username and password. (It's the same credentials you use to login to your SEAS email). Once you login, you will see additional menu items appear on the menu bar.

  • To see a list of rooms with detailed information and images, click on "Room Information".
  • To see room availability, scroll over "Reservations" and select "See Room Availability".
  • To make an express reservation with for immediate use with no special setup required, scroll over "Reservations" and select "Express Reservation".
  • To request a room (7 days prior to your event), scroll over "Reservations" and select "Reserve a Room".
  • To request the Dean's Conference Room, scroll over "Reservations" and select "Reserve the Dean's Conference Room".
  • To request the Brooks Room, scroll over "Reservations" and select "Reserve the Brooks Room".
  • To request the IACS Conference Room, scroll over "Reservations" and select "Reserve the IACS Conference Room".
  • To request the room Pierce Hall 301, scroll over "Reservations" and select "Reserve Pierce Hall 301".
  • To see your existing reservations, scroll over"Reservations" and select "View My Reservations".
Within 48 hours, you will receive an email notification on the status of your request.

If you have any questions about using the system, please contact the Computing helpdesk at help@seas.harvard.edu.

How do I reserve a room?

Scroll over "Reservations" in the menu above and select "Reserve a Room" to reserve a room 7 days prior to your event up until August 31st 2013. If your event requires more (or less) advanced planning or falls on a holiday, please contact events@seas.harvard.edu.  
If you wish to request a special room like the Dean's Conference Room, scroll over "Reservations" and select "Reserve the Dean's Conference Room".
You can also reserve the Brooks Room (scroll over "Reservations" and select "Reserve the Brooks Room"), the IACS Conference Room (scroll over "Reservations" and select "Reserve the IACS Conference Room") and Pierce Hall 301 ( scroll over "Reservations" and select "Reserve Pierce Hall 301").

Every room has its own set of rules. Please make sure to read them when you are about to reserve them. Please check the date of your reservation (e.g.: that you are reserving the room 7 days prior to the event or more), the time of your reservation (AM vs PM) and the attendance number (It won't show the room as available if your reservation attendance number exceed the room's maximum capacity).

How do I change my reservations?

Scroll over "Reservations" in the menu above and select "View My Reservations" to see your existing reservations. Once there, you can click on a reservation and request a change.

Please note that change requests must be submitted 4 business days prior to the event. We regret that we cannot accomodate any changes with less notice.

If you do not need a reservation anymore, please cancel the reservations in the same section, clicking on the link that says "Cancel Booking". Please note that if an event is due in 3 business days or less, you will need to contact events@seas.harvard.edu to request the cancellation.

Why can't I cancel my reservation?

The system won't let you cancel an event if an event is due in 3 business days or less. This is a policy established to allow the Events and Facilities Offices to have the necessary time to cancel the arrangements made for your event. To cancel an event that is 3 or less than 3 days away, you have to contact events@seas.harvard.edu to request the cancellation.

How do I make an express reservation?

Scroll over "Reservations" in the menu above and select "Express Reservation" and fill in your event details. There is no approval needed for an express reservation. You will receive an email confirming your reservation immediately after you have submitted your reservation.
Note: Please check the date of your reservation (e.g.: that you are reserving the room at least 7 days prior to the event or more and that it doesn't fall on a holiday), the time of your reservation (AM vs PM) and the attendance number (It won't show the room as available if your reservation attendance number exceed the room's maximum capacity).

I need to do a teleconference. What phone number do I use? What do I need?

If you click on "Room Information" in the menu above and then on a room name, the Room Details tab there will display the phone jacks and phone numbers enabled for them, if the room has any. Some of the rooms do not have phone jacks enabled or no phone jacks at all.
Most of the rooms do not have the teleconferencing phone in them, you have to request separately and in advance to SEAS Computing at help@seas.harvard.edu.
